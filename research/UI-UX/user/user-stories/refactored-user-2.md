# User Story 2: Student Intent vs. AI Interpretation View

## Story
As a student, I want to see a side-by-side view of my original instructions and the AI's current "plan of action," so that I can ensure the AI’s logic aligns with my goals.

## Why This Matters
To avoid confusion , the user needs to see how the AI is interpreting their direct input. This ensures that the human remains the director and can catch "hallucinations" or logical errors in the workflow early.

## Acceptance Criteria
- A "Plan Review" screen appears before the AI starts a task, requiring student approval
- The interface highlights potential areas where the AI's plan might deviate from the users's instructions.
- Users can edit the AI's "Internal Logic" or "Step-list" directly.

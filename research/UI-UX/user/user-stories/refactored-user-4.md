# User Story 2: Manual Override and Refinement

## Story
As a student, I want to be able to manually edit or refine any portion of the AI's work-in-progress, so that the final output reflects my own understanding and voice.

## Why This Matters
The AI shouldn't have the final say. To ensure the assignments are "correct" and not just "AI-generated slop," the student needs the ability to jump into the middle of the workflow and fix specific details manually.

## Acceptance Criteria
- "Edit Mode" allows the student to overwrite AI-generated text or code within the tracker.
- The AI tracks these manual edits as "Hard Constraints" for future tasks.
- A "Quality Score" or "Confidence Meter" shows how closely the AI followed the student's direct feedback.
